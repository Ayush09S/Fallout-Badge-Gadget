# Ayush's Fallout Badge Gadget
# Context
This project was created during Hack Club's 2026 Fallout Hackathon. It will be distributed to our friends before we leave, and is being paid for by the coupon provided to us from JLCPCB with an EasyEda Account. This was created on Day 2 of Fallout. 

# Design
This PCB design features multiple of my favorite hobbies and shows that I've watched. 

Shows include:
- The Amazing Digital Circus
- How To Train Your Dragon
- Hamilton
- Avatar The Last Airbender (NOT THE BLUE PEOPLE)
Hobbies include:
- Taekwondo (The picture is just a martial art)
- Robotics
- Hockey

I've also added some Hack Club assets, such as Soup, the Fallout logo and the Hack Club Flag.

The PCB can also activate LEDs that turn on through a switch. It is important to note that this PCB is designed to be without PCBA (without Assembly) and have components hand soldered.
